With season 7's finale, the latest chapter of Game of Thrones is finally concluded and the long wait for its 8th and final season begins. It was an explosive finale (though not as explosive as last year's finale if you follow along) with plenty of major plot points checked off and one somewhat major character death. This season was plagued with a lot of hindrances that seemed to let down a lot of fans. One major complaint was that the characters seemed to be able to travel instantly at will.
