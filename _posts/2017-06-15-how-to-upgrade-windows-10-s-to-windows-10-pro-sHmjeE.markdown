So your PC came pre-loaded with Windows 10 S and you want to install programs from outside the Store. Here's how to upgrade your Windows 10 S machine to Windows 10 Pro. Some devices come pre-installed with Windows 10 S, which is locked to the Windows Store for apps and games. Luckily, Microsoft offers Windows 10 S devices an upgrade path to Windows 10 Pro, unlocking the ability to install programs from the web. Here's how you can upgrade your own Windows 10 S device to Windows 10 Pro. How to upgrade Windows 10 S to Windows 10 Pro A PC running Windows 10 S can be easily upgraded to Windows 10 Pro. It takes just a few seconds, and on some devices may even be a free upgrade. In most cases however, the upgrade will cost $49.99. To initiate the upgrade process: Press the Start button located at the bottom left of your screen. Select the Settings icon, located just above the power icon on the Start menu.
