WTF happened to Ghost? According to the official record-keepers at the Citadel (or, more accurately, some observant fans over at the show's wikia page), the last time we saw Jon Snow's faithful direwolf on screen was in episode 3 of Season 6, "Oathbreaker," just after our favorite bastard was resurrected.
