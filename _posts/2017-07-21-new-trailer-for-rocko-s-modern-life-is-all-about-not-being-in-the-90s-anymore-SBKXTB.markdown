Your favourite animated wallaby from Australia is back, and he can't deal with 2017. Nickelodeon just unveiled a new trailer for Rocko's Modern Life: Static Cling , which debuted at San Diego Comic-Con. Can you believe it's been more than 20 years since the series ended? It'll feature the original voice actors behind Rocko, Heffer Wolfe, Filburt Turtle, Mrs. Fathead and others, thrust into the perplexing technological and social changes of the 21st century. Like a truly modern life, of course. The reboot will air as a TV movie in 2018, and we're betting that it's going to be a hoot. Read more...
