Winter is coming and Game of Thrones star Nikolaj Coster-Waldau really wants people to start taking climate change seriously. Coster-Waldau, who plays Jaime Lannister in the addictive HBO series, also happens to be a United Nations Development Program (UNDP) Goodwill Ambassador and passionate advocate for climate action. The 47-year-old spoke at Mashable 's 2017 Social Good Summit on Sunday to discuss the importance of fighting climate change and how it relates to the hit show.
