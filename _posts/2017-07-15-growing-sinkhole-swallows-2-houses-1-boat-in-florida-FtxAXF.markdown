The hole, which engulfed one home, a boat and then consumed about 80% of another home on Friday in Land O' Lakes, north of Tampa in Pasco County, has stretched up to 250 feet (76 meters) wide and 50 feet (15 meters) deep, and threatened to damage a third home, officials said.
