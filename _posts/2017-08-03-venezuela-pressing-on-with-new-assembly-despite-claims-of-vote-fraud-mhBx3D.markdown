Venezuelan President Nicolas Maduro is going ahead with the inauguration of a powerful new assembly, albeit with a 24-hour delay, even though the British firm hired to handle the vote said the turnout figure given by his government was too high. Attorney General Luisa Ortega, one of Maduro's most outspoken critics, said she has opened an investigation into what she called scandalous electoral fraud.
