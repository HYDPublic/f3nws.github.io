Harry Dean Stanton, a prolific actor with memorable roles in The Godfather: Part II , Alien , and Pretty in Pink , has died at age 91. Stanton most recently starred as Carl Rodd in the 2017 mystery drama series Twin Peaks. He is also known for his television role as an evil polygamist patriarch in Big Love.
