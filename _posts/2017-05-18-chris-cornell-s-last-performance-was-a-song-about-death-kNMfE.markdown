As if Chris Cornell's death wasn't shocking enough, it turns out the singer's final performance included singing about death. To close out Cornell's last show in Detroit, Soundgarden performed "Slaves and Bulldozers," an original song, mixed with Led Zeppelin's "In My Time of Dying." According to NME , this was a regular Soundgarden tradition, but it clearly takes on new significance after Cornell's death.
