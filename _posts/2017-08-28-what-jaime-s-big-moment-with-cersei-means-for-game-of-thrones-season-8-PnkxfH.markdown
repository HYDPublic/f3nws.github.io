Ah, Jaime Lannister. Longtime kingslayer, first-time queen betrayer. Turns out that most of the biggest political maneuvers in Game of Thrones Season 7 finale were a farce. That was one of the bombshell revelations from the episode's concluding sprint across Westeros: Cersei still gonna Cersei. She's been a vile, duplicitous monster for seven straight seasons and, hey... she's still got it! Although she agreed to stand beside Dany, Jon, and their assembled forces to fight in the Great War, Cersei never intended to do anything of the sort. Instead, she sent punk rock pirate boytoy Euron Greyjoy off on a secret mission to retrieve the Golden Company mercenaries: 20,000 warriors — with elephants — who can defeat dragons, I guess?
