iOS 11 has only been out a week but Apple has already released its first update. The company rolled out the 11.0.1 update Tuesday, which includes an important fix for a bug that was causing some users a major headache. SEE ALSO: 13 hidden features in iOS 11 The update fixes a bug that prevented some people who used Microsoft email accounts from sending messages, according to the company's support website . Specifically, the bug affected Outlook.com and Office 365 users who saw an error message when trying to send emails. Updating to iOS 11.0.1 should fix the issue, according to the company.
