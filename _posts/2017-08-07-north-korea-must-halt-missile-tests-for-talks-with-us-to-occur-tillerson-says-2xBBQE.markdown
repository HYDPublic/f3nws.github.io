Secretary of State Rex Tillerson said on Monday that North Korea must halt its missile testing if they want any sort of negotiations with the U.S. Tillerson -- who made the comments at a regional security meeting in Manilla, Philippines-- offered his most specific outline to date of what preconditions the U.S. has for talks with Pyongyang.
