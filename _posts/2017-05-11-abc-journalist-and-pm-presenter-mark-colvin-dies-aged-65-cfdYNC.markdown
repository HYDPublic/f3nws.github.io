One of the ABC's most respected, well-known and authoritative journalists, Mark Colvin, has died at the age of 65 after a long struggle with a rare auto-immune disease. Colvin's award-winning career spanned more than four decades, and for the last 20 years he was a familiar voice as the presenter of current affairs program PM.
