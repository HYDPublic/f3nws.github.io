These schoolboys are fighting their school's dress code, one skirt at a time. Around 50 boys at Isca Academy in Exeter, UK, protested their school's no shorts policy by turning up in skirts on Thursday, reported by Devon Live . The students reportedly got the idea from the school's head teacher, who jokingly suggested they could wear skirts if they like.
