Gerard Butler has taken to Facebook to share a moving video tribute to fellow actor Michael Nyqvist, who the BBC reports died Tuesday at the age of 56. Nyqvist was probably best known for his role as Mikael Blomkvist in The Girl with the Dragon Tattoo . He and Butler had recently starred together in the film Hunter Killer , which is currently in post-production.
