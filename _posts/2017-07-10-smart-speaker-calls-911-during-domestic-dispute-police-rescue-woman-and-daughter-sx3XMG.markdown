It can be a nuisance when a smart home device such as Google Home or Amazon Echo is inadvertently invoked. But it can also be a lifesaver. ABC News reported on Friday that a violent altercation between a man and his girlfriend was stopped after a smart home device called 911. He was allegedly armed with a firearm, physically attacked her and threatened to kill her.
