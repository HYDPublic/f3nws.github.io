In her new memoir Hunger , Roxane Gay details the everyday physical and psychological humiliations inflicted on her overweight body. If someone doesn't sneer at her size or comment on her food choices, they might pretend she doesn't exist at all, bumping and jostling her as they walk past. Never mind the persistent, ugly online harassment related to her size.
