DNA testing kits have been a game changer for people wanting to know more about their genetic makeup and ancestry. But, this technology has left all of us asking the exact same question: WHAT ABOUT THE DOGS?! Now, we can use a similar test on our canines. So, of course, we have cheek-swabbed a handful of pooches for genetic testing for Mashable 's brand new video series What's Your Mutt? The DNA test, which lets you identify breed, traits, and your doggo's genetic health risks, was conducted by the genetics company, Embark .
