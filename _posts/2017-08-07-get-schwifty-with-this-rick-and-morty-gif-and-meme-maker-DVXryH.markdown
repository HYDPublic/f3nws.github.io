If you're a fan of The Simpsons , you've likely long enjoyed Frinkiac , the search engine which whips up GIFs and memes from the animated series in a jiffy. Now the same people behind Frinkiac — Paul Kehrer, Sean Schulte, and Allie Young — have put together Master Of All Science , which does the same thing but with the also equally quotable Rick and Morty .
