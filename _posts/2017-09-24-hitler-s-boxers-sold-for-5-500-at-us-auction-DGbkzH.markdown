![Hitler's boxers sold for $5,500 at US auction](http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/photo/msid-60814896/60814896.jpg?35935)

The white striped linen under shorts are "surprisingly large", 19 inches long, with a waist of about 39 inches and bear Hitler's monogram: "A.H." just to the right of the top of the fly, according to Alexander Historical Auctions in US. The boxers were auctioned for $5,500, the auction house said.
