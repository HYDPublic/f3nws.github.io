First Justin Bieber, now Beyoncé. Beyoncé joined forces with reggaeton powerhouses J Balvin and Willy William for a remix of their popular hipshaker "Mi Gente" to raise money for Puerto Rico, Mexico, and Caribbean nations ravaged recently by natural disasters. "I am donating my proceeds from this song to hurricane relief charities for Puerto Rico, Mexico and the other affected Caribbean islands," she wrote on Instagram.
