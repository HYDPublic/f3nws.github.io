Can we please return to the stage of evolution when elephants, not humans, ruled the earth? A new video from the Save the Elephants Foundation features a group of elephants rushing to meet a new rescued baby elephant, "Dok Geaw," at Elephant Nature Park. Dok Geaw is almost two years old and far cuter than any human two-year-olds I know. Elephants are known for being deeply social and empathic creatures . It's not just anthropomorphism! Watch 'n cry.
