A new update to the native Photos app in Windows brings A.I. assisted searching of your OneDrive photos for easy reference. Here is how it works! Joining the ranks of Google's Photos app with intelligent image-sorting is Microsoft with its native Photos app for Windows 10 and Mobile. The company is evidently starting to roll out its own smart search that scans all your images on OneDrive and through artificial intelligence (A.I.) identifies and catalogs your photos based on categories, colors, months, and even faces. ...
