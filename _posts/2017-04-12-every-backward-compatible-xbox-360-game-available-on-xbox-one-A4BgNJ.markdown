Here's a list of every Xbox 360 game you can play on your Xbox One. Updated April 2017: Added the latest additions to backward compatibility, including the highly anticipated Call of Duty: Black Ops II. When the Xbox One originally launched in 2013, the lack of backward compatibility raised quite the uproar among fans. With the Xbox 360 having supported a majority of original Xbox games, it became a frequent request from fans (despite the technical limitations). The feature's return was later announced at E3 2015, allowing a select range of Xbox 360 titles to run on the Xbox One. With a growing library of titles to support the feature, we compiled a list of every backward compatible game.
