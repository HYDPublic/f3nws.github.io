At last, Bill Gates has joined us in the present. The Microsoft co-founder announced in an interview with Fox News Sunday that he's now using an Android phone. The move was probably inevitable. Windows on mobile has effectively been a dead platform ever since Microsoft gutted its phone division two years ago. While Windows 10 technically works on phones, there's been pretty much zero support from hardware makers and app developers outside of Microsoft. Gates told Fox that the phone he uses is an "Android phone with a lot of Microsoft software." This could suggest that he's using the Microsoft Edition of the Samsung Galaxy S8 , or perhaps he's just downloaded Microsoft's lineup of apps, available across Android systems. Read more...
