CARACAS, Venezuela (AP) — The official count of Venezuelans who participated in the election of an all-powerful constituent assembly was tampered with — off by at least 1 million votes — the head of a voting technology company asserted Wednesday, a finding certain to sow further discord over the super-body that has generated months of nationwide protests.
