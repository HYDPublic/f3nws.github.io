Republican Sen. John Cornyn of Texas on Thursday defended the GOP’s proposed health care bill against the charge that millions of Americans would lose coverage under it by pointing out it would be their own choice. Cornyn responded to a tweet that he was willing to trade 250,000 jobs for 22 million losing coverage by writing, “Not lose, choose. When Bloomberg reporter Steven Dennis pointed out that “[+22 million] won’t be ‘losing’ health insurance, they’ll be ‘choosing’ to go without” was a frequent GOP talking point in the Senate hallways, Cornyn responded.
