Hugh Hefner, the founder of Playboy magazine, died at his home in The Playboy Mansion in Los Angeles, California at the age of 91, Playboy has confirmed. Hefner founded Playboy along with his associates in 1953, and the company went on to become an immensely popular brand publishing one of the most well-known men's magazines.
