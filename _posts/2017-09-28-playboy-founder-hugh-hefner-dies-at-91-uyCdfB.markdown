Hugh Hefner, founder of Playboy magazine and cultural provocateur who pushed sexuality into the mainstream in the 50s and 60s long before it was accepted, died at his famed Playboy Mansion home in Los Angeles, Playboy has confirmed. He was 91. Hefner founded Playboy along with his associates in 1953, and the company went on to become an immensely popular brand publishing one of the most well-known men's magazines.
