Pakistan today hoisted the largest flag in its history at Attari-Wagah border near Lahore to mark the country's 70th Independence Day. Pakistan Army Chief Gen. Qamar Javed Bajwa hoisted the flag at the border at 12 in the midnight, kicking off the festivities of Independence Day across the country
