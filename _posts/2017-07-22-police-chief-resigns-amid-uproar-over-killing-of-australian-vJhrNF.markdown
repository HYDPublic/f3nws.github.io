The police chief in Minneapolis resigned Friday amid an uproar over the death of an Australian woman who had called to report a possible sexual assault and was shot by a responding officer. Chief Janee Harteau faced criticism over her handling of the incident last Saturday night, which ignited an international outcry. Minneapolis Mayor Betsy Hodges said she asked for the chief's resignation Friday and Harteau tendered it.
