Residents of the Florida Keys ravaged by Hurricane Irma, which has left at least 12 dead statewide, discovered scenes of desolation as they returned home Tuesday amid a massive operation to restore electricity to millions of people still without power in three southern US states. As emergency workers picked through the rubble, officials put Irma's death toll in Florida at 12 -- counting 10 more dead than on Monday. The storm has now killed at least 50 people across the Caribbean and the United States.
