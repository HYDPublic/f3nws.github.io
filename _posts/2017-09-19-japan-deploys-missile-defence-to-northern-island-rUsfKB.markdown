Japan will on Tuesday deploy an additional missile defence system on its northern island of Hokkaido, a defence ministry spokesman told AFP, days after North Korea launched a missile over the island. Japan has already deployed the Patriot Advanced Capability-3 missile defence system to another part of Hokkaido.
