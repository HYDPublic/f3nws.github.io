Home rain catching is a pretty slick affair these days, no longer relegated to the realms of industrial water towers. This new Dutch design is the newest backyard version of a trusty ol' water tank, a water-saving device dubbed the Raindrop . SEE ALSO: How your morning cup of coffee can help save the world The Raindrop is essentially an enhanced rain barrel, designed by Dutch designer Bas van de Veer as a graduation project in 2009 and produced by Dutch pottery company Elho in 2011.
