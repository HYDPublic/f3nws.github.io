Donald Trump has a Twitter problem. In addition to his penchant for delivering surreal, stream-of-consciousness rants at any time of day on the microblogging platform, Donald Trump can't seem to stop deleting tweets. Many times, his tweets are deleted because of typos, including the infamous "covfefe" tweet . But the reasons behind deleting other examples, like one about his alleged appearance on Fox & Friends, remain a mystery.
