Android has an emoji problem . No, it's not those weird blobs ( RIP ), but something that's been nearly as tricky to fix. Because of Android handset makers' inconsistent updates, not only do many Android users not get new software updates in a timely manner, they don't get new emoji at the same time as their iPhone-using counterparts. SEE ALSO: After 10 years, I'm breaking up with Android While Google will probably never really fix its fragmentation problem , the company is working on a fix for the emoji issue, as highlighted Monday by Emojipedia .
