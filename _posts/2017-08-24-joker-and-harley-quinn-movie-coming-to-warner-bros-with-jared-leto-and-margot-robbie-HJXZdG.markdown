Certifiably crazy, possibly stupid, definitely love. Warner Bros. is moving ahead with a "Joker and Harley Quinn" movie with Margot Robbie and Jared Leto reprising their Suicide Squad roles — and the directing duo of Glenn Ficarra and John Requa ( Crazy, Stupid, Love ) are onboard to helm it. THR broke the news, which WB had yet to confirm Tuesday. Ficarra and Requa, the directors and executive producers of This Is Us , are in final talks to both helm and write, THR hollywood reports. But maybe don't hold your breath — word is that Harley and Joker would come after the sequel to Suicide Squad , which is struggling mightily to land a director; whoever that is will have a helluva time hitting the film's 2019 target.
