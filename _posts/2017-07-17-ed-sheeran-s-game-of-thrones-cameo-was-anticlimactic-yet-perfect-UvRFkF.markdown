Spoiler warning: This post contains plot details for the Game of Thrones Season 7 premiere, titled "Dragonstone." Turns out that Ed Sheeran was telling the truth about his Game of Thrones cameo , in which he appeared alongside Maisie Williams' Arya Stark, because even Game of Thrones stars are fans of stuff . "I feel like my cameo’s been built up too much though, people will just be like 'Oh. Oh, alright,'" he told ET last month. "It’s not an integral part of it at all, I’m just, like… in it." We all thought he was kidding when he revealed what he'd be doing during the scene in a radio interview: "I sing a song and then [Arya] goes, 'Oh, that's a nice song,' and I go, 'It's a new one,' and then there's other lines."
