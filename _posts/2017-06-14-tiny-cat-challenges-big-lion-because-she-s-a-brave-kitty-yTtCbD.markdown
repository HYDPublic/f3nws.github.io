Oh, tiny cats. You have the DNA of the big cats, so why not challenge them as if they're your equals? YouTube user BigCatDerek uploaded a video of his cat Baggy challenging a lion named Noey who lives in his animal rescue facility. It's like the cat version of David and Goliath, if the two were separated by a wire fence. "Baggy," her owner called out. "You will lose!" But she didn't listen.
