President Donald Trump paid a visit to Yad Vashem, Israel's national Holocaust memorial on Tuesday, where he composed an upbeat and strangely optimistic note in the memorial's guestbook. “IT IS A GREAT HONOR TO BE HERE WITH ALL OF MY FRIENDS — SO AMAZING & WILL NEVER FORGET!” Trump wrote in all caps. Yes, he basically brought a happy birthday card to a funeral.
