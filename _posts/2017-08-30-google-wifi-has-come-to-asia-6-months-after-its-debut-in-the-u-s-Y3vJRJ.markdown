UPDATE: Aug. 30, 2017, 4:29 p.m. SGT Google has launched Hong Kong availability, in addition to Singapore. Google Wifi has launched in Asia, making mesh Wi-Fi easier for the average man on the street — but there's a catch. The Wi-Fi router package is now available in Singapore and Hong Kong. But for its Asian debut, Google Wifi is going to be a little harder to access, compared with Western countries.
