Jon Snow and Daenerys Targaryen finally met in this week's Game of Thrones — a union that fans of George R. R. Martin's books have been anticipating for... well, basically the past 20 years, since the first novel in his A Song of Ice and Fire series was published in 1996. Showrunners David Benioff and Dan Weiss give us some insight into Jon and Dany's much-anticipated face-off in a behind-the-scenes segment after the episode, revealing that, after years of hype, both characters have a few preconceptions that they have to work through.
