A new rumor from leaker Benjamin Geskin says Apple's upcoming OLED iPhone — or iPhone 8, if that's what it'll be called — will come in four colors, including an entirely new one: "mirror." Geskin shared no details about the new color, and the photos he supplied were photos of an iPhone in a mirror-colored case, not photos of the iPhone 8.
