By David Beasley ATLANTA (Reuters) - Chantek, a male orangutan who was among the first apes to learn sign language, could clean his room and memorized the way to a fast-food restaurant, died on Monday at age 39, Zoo Atlanta said. Chantek, who was taught by a trainer who raised him like her own child, was being treated for heart disease, the zoo said in a statement. "Chantek will be deeply missed by his family here at Zoo Atlanta.
